HOW TO PLAY

Simple Rule. Deep Strategy.

It's pretty simple once you get the hang of it:

  • Slide your finger across empty squares

  • Place gems in the drawn path

  • Match at least two identical crystals

  • Clear the grid completely

At the top of the grid, you’ll see the gemstone sequence. Each time you complete a line, the order changes, requiring you to adapt and plan carefully.
Each move you make affects what happens next, so you've gotta think ahead.
